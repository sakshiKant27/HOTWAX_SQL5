## 10. Total Orders by Sales Channel
Business Problem:   
Marketing and sales teams want to see how many orders come from each channel (e.g., web, mobile app, in-store POS, marketplace) to allocate resources effectively.  
Fields to Retrieve:  
SALES_CHANNEL  
TOTAL_ORDERS  
TOTAL_REVENUE  
REPORTING_PERIOD
